## What we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect: your name, email address, optionally your company name, and the contents of your message. We also collect standard HTTP server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp) generated automatically by our hosting provider, Netlify.

## Why we collect it

To respond to your inquiry. We do not use this data for marketing, profiling, or advertising.

## Where it is stored

Form submissions are stored by Netlify (our hosting provider, based in the United States) and emailed to us. Server logs are retained by Netlify according to their own retention policies.

## How long we keep it

We keep form submissions for as long as needed to resolve your inquiry, then delete them. You can request deletion at any time.

## Your rights

Under GDPR (if you are in the EU) and 152-ФЗ (if you are in Russia), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to processing. Contact us at hello@361robotics.com to exercise these rights.

## Updates

If we change this policy, we will update this page and note the change date.
